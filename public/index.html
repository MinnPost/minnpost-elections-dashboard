2022 Election Results Dashboard

Oct. 21, 2022

The front page of this dashboard shows some of the state’s most closely-watched races. Results are available as they are reported by the Minnesota Secretary of State. Initial election results are expected after polls close at 8 p.m. on Election Day, November 8. For ranked-choice vote elections, final results will be available after tabulations are released by the governments conducting those elections.
